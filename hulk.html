Hulk

Biography

Bruce Banner is a brilliant scientist who, after a gamma radiation accident, transforms into the Hulk when he experiences anger or stress. The Hulk is a green-skinned, hulking and muscular humanoid possessing a vast degree of physical strength and durability.
