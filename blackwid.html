Black Widow

Biography

Natasha Romanoff, also known as Black Widow, is a highly trained spy and assassin. She is a former KGB agent and a member of the Avengers.

Alias: Black Widow

Occupation: Spy, Assassin

Place of Birth: Stalingrad, USSR
